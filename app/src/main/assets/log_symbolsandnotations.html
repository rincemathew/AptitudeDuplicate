Question:If * stands for 'addition', / stands for 'subtraction', + stands for 'multiplication', and - stands for 'division', then 20 * 8 / 8 - 4 + 2 = ? Answer:Using the correct symbols, we have: Given expression = 20 + 8 - 8 / 4 * 2 = 20 + 8 - 2 * 2 = 20 + 8 - 4 = 24.
