Additions/Subtractions
The addition of two whole numbers is the total amount of those values combined.
Subtraction is an arithmetic operation that represents the operation of removing objects from a collection. The result of a subtraction is called a difference. Subtraction is signified by the minus sign (−).
Multiplications
The multiplication of two numbers is equivalent to adding as many copies of one of them
Percentages
A percentage is a number or ratio that represents a fraction of 100. It is often denoted by the symbol "%"
