ODD MAN OUT

Odd Man out: A person or thing that is different from or kept apart from others that form a group or set.

Question:Find the odd one:
2,3,5,7,10,11,13
Answer:Each of the number except 10, is a prime number. So '10' is the odd one

Question:Find the odd one:
Few,Big,Small,Large,Tiny
Answer:The odd one is 'Few'. Because, Few is related to number and others are related to size.

Question:Find the odd one:
Chair,Table,Cot,Furniture,Stool
Answer:Everything execpt Furniture is a type of Furniture. So 'Furniture' is the odd one.
