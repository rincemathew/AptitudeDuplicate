Mensuration Formulae

Question:What is the Circumference of a Circle with Diameter of 100m?
Answer:Circumference = π * Diameter
Circumference = π * 100
Circumference = 314m

Question:Find the Area of Square of side 5cm?
Answer:Area of Square = (side)2
Area of Square = 5 * 5
Area of Square = 25cm2
